🎲 How to Play

🎯 Goal

Score the most points by rolling dice and building the best combinations. Each category can only be used once.

🔁 A Turn
  • Roll all 5 dice
  • Click any dice to hold them
  • Re-roll the rest — up to 3 rolls total
  • Click a scoring category to lock in your score
🧠 The Key Idea

After your first roll, pick a goal and keep the dice that help you reach it. Don't hold random dice — build toward something.

Example: Roll 3 3 5 2 6 → keep the 3s and aim for 3 of a Kind, or keep 2 3 5 6 and chase a Straight.

  • Numbers in sequence → aim for a Straight
  • Bad roll → use Chance or sacrifice a low-value slot
  • Don't waste good categories on bad rolls
